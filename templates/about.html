Our Story

Little Lemon is a family-owned restaurant specializing in lemon-inspired dishes. We started with a small idea and a big love for fresh, flavorful ingredients. Today, we offer a menu that combines tradition with a twist of creativity, all while celebrating the bright and zesty flavor of lemon!

Why Choose Us?

At Little Lemon, we believe in serving fresh, high-quality food made with love and care. Whether you're here for a light lunch, a refreshing drink, or a memorable dinner, we promise you'll enjoy every bite. Our chefs are passionate about experimenting with lemon and bringing out its natural zest in every dish!

Join Us Today!

Experience the zest and freshness that only Little Lemon can offer. Come visit us or make a reservation today!
